  • Purpose of the event: To fundraise money for the Pediatric Trauma Program (PTP). The Pediatric Trauma Program was developed because unintentional injury kills more children every year than any other cause. 90% of these injuries could have been prevented. PTP develop local projects, which will reduce the number of children in California, Nevada, and Hawaii who are killed or injured by trauma.The purpose of PTP is to educate parents about how to prevent accidental injury or death, and to train doctors on how to treat children that have been injured due to accidental causes.
